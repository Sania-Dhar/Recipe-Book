Paneer Butter Masala

  • Paneer - 200g
  • Butter - 2 tbsp
  • Tomato puree - 1 cup
  • Cream - 1/2 cup
  • Spices & salt
Sauté onion, ginger-garlic in butter. Add tomato puree, spices. Cook until thick. Add cream and paneer. Simmer till soft. Garnish with cream and coriander. Rich, buttery, and flavorful, this dish pairs best with naan or jeera rice. Serve hot for a perfect restaurant-style North Indian curry experience.

Chole Masala

  • Boiled chickpeas - 2 cups
  • Onions - 2
  • Tomatoes - 2
  • Ginger-garlic paste - 1 tbsp
  • Spices & oil
Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger. Add tomatoes and spices. Cook into thick masala. Add boiled chickpeas and simmer. Garnish with coriander and lemon. Serve hot with bhature or rice. This hearty, spiced chickpea curry is a popular Punjabi dish that’s perfect for festive lunches and comfort dinners alike.

Vegetable Biryani

  • Basmati rice - 1.5 cups
  • Mixed veggies - 1.5 cups
  • Yogurt - 1/2 cup
  • Biryani masala - 2 tbsp
  • Fried onions, saffron, mint
Sauté onions and spices. Add veggies, yogurt, biryani masala. Cook rice separately. Layer rice and veggies with saffron, mint, and fried onions. Cover tightly and cook on dum. Let rest before serving. This aromatic, flavorful one-pot meal is a vegetarian feast. Serve hot with raita or salan curry.

Aloo Gobi

  • Potatoes - 2
  • Cauliflower florets - 2 cups
  • Tomato - 1
  • Ginger - 1 tsp
  • Spices & coriander
Sauté cumin, onion, and ginger. Add tomato and spices. Stir in chopped potatoes and cauliflower. Cover and cook until vegetables are soft. Garnish with fresh coriander and lemon. A simple, dry curry with earthy flavors. Pairs best with roti or rice. A comfort food classic loved in every home.
